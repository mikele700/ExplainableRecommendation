Dayz? Step aside. This game allows to immerse youself in the world of the wretched zombies. You as an unturned human must scavenge the abandoned islands to survive, thrive and escape. With the next generation of flawless graphics, awesome AIs and insane items. If that is not enough, there is multiplayer mode which allows you and your friends to go through the experience of an apocalytic event together!The games is still under developement but think about what it would be when it is complete! 10/10.
